2.) Information On GANs

Description: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) are a class of machine learning models that consist of two sub-models: a generator and a discriminator. The generator produces synthetic data that is intended to resemble real-world data, while the discriminator attempts to distinguish between the synthetic data and real-world data. The two sub-models are trained in an adversarial manner, with the generator trying to produce synthetic data that can fool the discriminator, and the discriminator trying to accurately distinguish between synthetic and real data.
GANs have been used to generate a wide range of data, including images, text, and audio. They have been used in applications such as image synthesis, super-resolution, style transfer, and text-to-speech. GANs have also been used to improve the performance of other machine learning models, such as in the fields of computer vision and natural language processing.
It is important to conduct a comprehensive literature review on GANs for several reasons: GANs are a relatively new and rapidly evolving field, with new developments and improvements being made on a regular basis. A literature review can provide an overview of the state-of-the-art in GANs and help identify potential areas for future research. GANs have been applied to a wide range of fields and applications. A literature review can help identify the most promising and impactful applications of GANs and the specific challenges that need to be addressed. GANs have many hyperparameters that can be adjusted, and it is important to understand the impact of these parameters on the performance of GANs. A literature review can provide insights into the best practices for training GANs and the trade-offs between different hyperparameter settings. GANs are a challenging model to train, and there are many challenges that need to be addressed such as mode collapse and stability of the training process. A literature review can help identify the best practices for addressing these challenges and the latest research on these challenges. Overall, GANs have shown to be a powerful tool for various systems and applications, and a comprehensive literature review will help to understand the impact of GANs in various systems and applications as well as the challenges that need to be addressed..
